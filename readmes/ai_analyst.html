✦ Core Features

Every angle of a deal.
One intelligent platform.

Five purpose-built AI modules that replace weeks of analyst work with actionable intelligence delivered in minutes.

📄
01 / AI Analyzer
Pitch Deck Intelligence
Upload any pitch deck PDF. Our AI extracts key strategic insights, financial metrics, risk flags, and delivers a buy/invest verdict with full citations. Tables and charts are reconstructed automatically.
📊
02 / Competitor Analysis
Market Landscape Radar
Input any two companies for an instant head-to-head analysis — market cap, pricing strategy, tech moat, and SWOT — with a definitive investment verdict backed by live web data.
🎥
03 / Video Pitch Analyzer
Founder Credibility Score
Paste a YouTube link. AI watches the pitch, scores founder confidence, extracts business claims, fact-checks every number against live sources, and flags visual red flags instantly.

Natural Language Analytics
Connect your MongoDB and ask questions in plain English. No SQL needed. Get instant summaries, auto-generated charts, and investment recommendations from your own proprietary data.
💬

RAG-Powered Q&A
Upload pitch decks, financial reports, or contracts. Ask anything in plain English. Our RAG engine surfaces precise answers with citations — no hallucinations, just evidence-backed responses.

Visual Data Extraction
Deep dive into massive PDF files. Extract embedded tables, reconstruct charts, summarize multi-hundred-page documents, and identify anomalies in seconds using Gemini Vision.
✦ How It Works

From raw data to
conviction in minutes.

A single intelligent pipeline that ingests any source and returns structured, actionable intelligence.

01
Ingest any data source
Upload pitch deck PDFs, paste YouTube links, connect your MongoDB, or provide competitor URLs. Any format, any source.
02
Multi-modal processing
Cloud Vision OCR extracts text from documents. YouTube Transcript API captures spoken words. Crawl4AI scrapes competitor sites in real time.
03
Gemini AI Engine
Vertex AI serves Gemini 3.0 Pro for deep reasoning. Entity extraction, semantic scoring, and NLP processing transforms raw content into structured intelligence.
04
Grounded investment verdict
Every claim is fact-checked against live Google Search. Outputs are stored in ChromaDB + MongoDB and delivered as an interactive dashboard with JSON export.
